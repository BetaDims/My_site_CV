Electric Vehicle — Mechanical design project

In the mechanical design course (senior year), teams of ten people are formed to carry out a design project under the German design standard VDI 2221. In my case, the project was an electric vehicle and my role was to document the project from beginning to end and take measurements of the electrical circuit that was implemented in the electrical vehicle (voltage, continuity and current).

  • Documentation was done from start to finish: Calculations, 3D modeling and manufacturing process.
  • Measuring tools were used such as: Vernier, Multi-tester.
  • It was made public through different media outlets - news article
  • The project was left to the students to continue adding new features to the vehicle.

Tools: SolidWorks, Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Powerpoint)
