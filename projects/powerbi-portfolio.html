Project 03 · Data & Visualization

Power BI — Project Portfolio Dashboard

A single executive view of every active project — purchase orders, follow-up activities and KPIs — pulled together so leadership could see budget and execution status in real time instead of chasing spreadsheets.

Role
Project Assistant
Tool
Microsoft Power BI
Type
Personal / Internal
Focus
PO & KPI tracking
01

The Problem

Project status lived in too many places at once.

Purchase orders sat in one spreadsheet, follow-up activities in another, and progress in someone's inbox. Answering a simple question — how much have we committed, and where do things stand? — meant stitching files together by hand every time leadership asked.

One question, three spreadsheets, every time. The dashboard ended that.

02

What It Tracks

◷
Purchase Orders
Total committed amount per project and per site, with status roll-ups so nothing slips between the cracks.
◆
Follow-up Activities
Open items, owners and due states — the operational to-do layer behind each project.
▲
Project KPIs
Execution and budget indicators that surface which projects are healthy and which need attention.
◰
Multi-site View
Filter across every active site at once, or drill into a single one — same dashboard, one click.
03

Impact

The dashboard replaced a recurring manual reporting chore with a live view leadership could open themselves. Budget commitments and execution status became answerable in seconds — and the conversation shifted from “what's the number?” to “what do we do about it?”
