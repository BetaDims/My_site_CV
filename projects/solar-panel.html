Overview

This is the project that sits closest to my degree — pure mechanical engineering, applied to energy.

A solar install is deceptively physical: panel orientation drives yield, the mounting structure has to survive wind and weight, and every bracket is a small structural problem. I ran it as a complete project, owning each stage from the first site visit to the panels producing power.

Scope of Work

01Site SurveyAssessed the location, orientation and shading to size the system and find the optimal panel placement.
02Layout DesignPlanned the array layout for maximum exposure within the available area and structural limits.
03Structural MountingWorked through the mounting and structural considerations so the array carries its load safely in real conditions.
04On-site SupervisionOversaw the physical installation through to a working, commissioned system.
